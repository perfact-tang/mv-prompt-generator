# Wan2.2 Video Generation Prompt Guide

## Core Formula
**Prompt = Subject + Scene + Motion + Aesthetic + Style**

### 1. Subject (Who/What)
*   **Description**: Detailed appearance (clothing, features).
*   *Example*: "A black-haired Miao girl in ethnic dress"

### 2. Scene (Where)
*   **Description**: Environment, background, lighting, weather.
*   *Example*: "Standing on a misty mountain peak, surrounded by ancient stone carvings"

### 3. Motion (Action)
*   **Description**: Specific movement, speed, amplitude.
*   *Example*: "Slowly turning around, sleeves dancing in the wind"

### 4. Aesthetic (Look)
*   **Lighting**: Sunlight, Moonlight, Artificial, Rim Light, Volumetric.
*   **Camera**: Close-up, Wide shot, Low angle, Drone view.
*   **Lens**: Bokeh, Anamorphic, Macro.
*   *Example*: "Backlit, warm tones, cinematic lighting"

### 5. Style (Vibe)
*   **Art Style**: Cyberpunk, Watercolor, Anime, Photorealistic, Film Noir.
*   *Example*: "Ink wash painting style"

---

## Key Control Dimensions

### Lighting
*   **Natural**: Golden hour, Overcast, Sunny.
*   **Cinematic**: Rembrant lighting, Silhouette, Neon (Cyberpunk).

### Camera Movement
*   **Basic**: Pan (Left/Right), Tilt (Up/Down), Zoom (In/Out).
*   **Advanced**: Tracking shot, FPV Drone, Orbit/Arc, Handheld (shaky).

### Motion Keywords
*   **Subtle**: Breathing, blinking, hair blowing.
*   **Dynamic**: Running, dancing, fighting, exploding.
*   **Fluid**: Water flowing, smoke swirling.

## Prompting Tips for MV
1.  **Continuity**: Use the "Last Frame" logic for consecutive shots. Mention the same subject features.
2.  **Emotion**: Describe facial expressions ("teary-eyed", "joyful").
3.  **Rhythm**: Match motion speed to music (Slow motion for ballads, fast cuts for rock).
